Our Services

Flight Reservations


As an IATA, ASATA and ANTA accredited travel agency Trippers offers cost effective flight reservations to all destinations. Preference for any particular airline is of course taken into consideration whenever reservations are made. Trippers’s association with Tourvest Travel Services gives Trippers a firm representation in the international arena.

Accommodation


Trippers takes pride in giving the customers a choice of their specific accommodation needs, no matter where – regionally, nationally, and internationally. Certain corporate discounts are available, with preferred accommodation establishments (with bill back facilities).

Car Hire


Trippers is an agent for a number of internationally recognized car rental companies. A mutually beneficial relationship exists with Europcar, Budget and Avis from with special rates which are passed onto our customers. However, should the customer prefer any other car rental company, Trippers will oblige.

Delivery service


In Windhoek and Swakopmund, Trippers delivers airline tickets and itinerary travel documents, free of charge, to its customers, should they so wish.

Incentive programme and other special services


Special programs sports tours, staff incentive programs, conferencing, etc. can be provided for. This could include soccer, rugby or any other sports packages.

Airline Loyalty


Incentives earned through airline programs are passed onto the customers. Trippers would like to ensure that all travelers are in possession of the royalty programs with the airlines. For business class travels, Trippers ensures the usage of airport lounges to make all customers’ trip as comfortable as humanly possible.

Leisure Travel


Trippers provides a complete leisure travel section. The main purpose of this is to serve the exotically tropical islands such as Mauritius , Seychelles and Madagascar .

Team Building experiences


Trippers offers unforgettable team building experiences that include a wide range of activities such as 4×4 trips undertaken between Luderitz and Walvis Bay.

Specials


Airline packages or any other specials are passed on to our customers. As a common practice at Trippers, Trippers communicates the “specials” to the customers whenever the specials are received from the airlines.

Visas


Trippers has a department which offers professional and timeous visa services. The department also advises clients regarding visa requirements and/or any other information relevant to a specific destination.

Travel insurance


Trippers provides travel insurance policies provided by AIG, Hollard and TIC.

Emergency Services


Trippers provides a 24 hour, 7 day a week emergency service to all customers.

Accounting services


Trippers issues an invoice to the customer on receipt of a customer’s instruction and post the following day. Car hire, tours, hotel, etc., are billed on receipt of the supplier’s invoice. Statements reflecting full travel particulars are posted on the 2nd day of each month. Statements are payable within seven (7) days from the date of the issued statement. A customer control account can be generated whenever requested.
